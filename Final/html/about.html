How it Works

The top piece of the two part wall mount system works by a clip in system which uses a front support and rear support system that locks the book in, taking advantage of the CGC case's ridge design.

(Support)

How it Works

The bottom piece of the two part wall mount system works by adding additional support to the top piece incase of any incidences, such as kids throwing balls in the house, curious animals, and earthquakes.
