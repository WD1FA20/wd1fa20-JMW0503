FAQ's

  • How much does each set cost?
  • Each set costs $6 including a, top piece, bottom piece, 2 drywall anchors, and 2 screws specifically chosen for the mounts design.
  • How much does each piece weigh?
  • Each piece weighs 2 grams.
  • How are they made?
  • Each set is made from a polyeurathane resin poured into moulds. Each mould and pour is measured, mixed, and poured by hand. Once the parts are ready to come out of the moulds, they are hand sanded to get a perfect finished product.
